Climbing

A friend and I picked up climbing as something to get us out of the house as the peak of the pandemic was winding down. I fell in love with the sport. I prefer bouldering and have competed in a friendly bouldering competetition, in which my team took first place. I love the self improvement aspect of the sport: the only opposition I have is me, so I find it fun to push myself to constantly get better. The highest I've climbed is a V7, but that was a bit of a fluke in my opinion, as I just kinda jumped to the top using my height, so I like to say the highest I've climbed is a V6.
